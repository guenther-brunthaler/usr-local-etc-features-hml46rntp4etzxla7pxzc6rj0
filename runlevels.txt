Runlevels defined for this installation:

0 "Halt": Shut down system. Do not use directly.
1 Internal helper for runlevels 0 or 6. Do not use directly.
S "Single-User Mode": Work on the system console only.
2 "setup": Stop all services except Ethernet LAN networking.
3 "nonetsvc": Multi-user mode with SSH but no other net services.
4 "network": Multi-user mode with all networking services.
5 "x11": like runlevel 4, but with GUI. No other runlevel has GUI.
6 "Reboot": Reboot the system
